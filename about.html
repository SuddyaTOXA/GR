Greyrock’s Direct Fuel Production™ (DFP) systems convert waste gases, biomass or waste, natural gas or natural gas liquids (eg. ethane or propane) directly into premium liquid fuels. Feedstocks can be varied, facilitating reduced cost of inputs. Products can also be tailored to respond to market opportunities. Unlike complex gas-to-liquids (GTL) or biomass-to-liquid (BTL) plants, Greyrock DFP™ plants are simple and modular using the Greycat TM catalyst to convert various feedstocks into liquid fuels without ever going into the wax phase, which eliminates the need fro complex refining steps found in typical Fishcer Tropsch based plants. Greyrock plants can operate economically in much lower oil environment than typical conversion plants because of the reduced CAPEX and OPEX associated with simple plant configurations and operations.

To deliver the most holistic and complete package to work in every sector, Greyrock’s management team and highly interactive board offers vast and diverseexperience from commercial, engineering, and operations in various sectors including upstream, midstream, downstream, power and utilities, motor vehicle design, motor vehicle technology, intellectual property, mining and metals, catalytic science, and manufacturing. Combining experience from these fields allows Greyrock to deliver on the specific needs of every industry as it relates to gas conversion, fuel generation, and fuel consumption.

Uneconomic gas conversion is a thing of the past. Contact Us today to see how Greyrock can service your needs.
