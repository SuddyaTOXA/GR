Movable Systems and Fixed Plants

Greyrock’s Direct Fuel ProductionTM platform enables the conversion of natural gas or waste resources into clean liquid transportation fuels. Sizes range from 5 barrels per day to 5000 barrels per day. Our award winning system design is a game changer in how the world produces liquid transportation fuels. M-Class systems are skid mounted, movable systems ideal for a natural gas well pad, flare gas site or waste gas application. Our fixed plants come in standardized sizes including 5000, 2500, 450 barrels per day.

  • Use Varied Feedstocks

    Greyrock systems can accept a variety of input feedstocks. Abundant natural gas and natural gas liquids (ethane, propane, etc.) are ideal in oversupplied markets. In addition, waste gases from industrial processes and even waste biomass can be used as feedstocks into Greyrock plants to produce fuels.

  • Produce Premium Fuel Products

    Systems produce diesel fuel with premium prop such as no sulfur, high cetane, and good lubricity, and a gasoline blendstock which is a premium blending stock for the highest tiers of gasoline. Other products such as kerosene, hydrogen, and steam can also be isolated and sold based on market availability.

    Greyrock Catalyst

    Greyrock’s catalyst eliminates the “wax production” step associated with traditional Fischer-Tropsch. When wax is produced using traditional Fischer-Tropsch this wax needs to be refined into finished liquid fuels. This refining step requires large infrastructure with high capital and operating expenses.
